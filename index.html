Team Feedback

A great new tool to develop teams and companies

Team Feedback is a new kind of corporate feedback tool which makes giving and receiving feedback a great simple routine.

Get started Get started

For teams up to 100 people TeamFeedback is free of charge.

Introduction

What is Team Feedback

For your employees it’s the new simple way to give and to receive feedback. For your team leaders it’s a very efficient tool to develop their teams. For your company it’s the new database of your people’s and team’s development.

For your employees

The new simple way to give feedback

Let your people give each other feedback with just a few clicks. Very simple, very quick and by the way it makes fun. Most given feedback is based on feedback routines which appear for users as a simple todo list. For example giving feedback to five colleagues takes just around a minute.
