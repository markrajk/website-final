Introduction

What is Team Feedback

For your employees it’s the new simple way to give and to receive feedback. For your team leaders it’s a very efficient tool to develop their teams. For your company it’s the new database of your people’s and team’s development.

For your employees

The new simple way
to give feedback

Let your people give each other feedback with just a few clicks. Very simple, very quick and by the way it makes fun. Most given feedback is based on feedback routines which appear for users as a simple todo list. For example giving feedback to five colleagues takes just around a minute. With team feedbacks team leaders collect their peoples opinion about how do they feel working in their team and what do they think could be done better in their team. With the default settings Team feedbacks are automatically collected every second week. With team leader feedback employees give anonymously feedback about their teamleader. Do you feel your boss treats you fairly? Does he plan the work well enough? Does he give clear enough instructions? Let your people give each other feedback with just a few clicks. For your team leaders

A very efficient tool to develop teams

Through these new feedback routines, a lot of great data is created. A great new data source for team leaders to follow their teams’ and employees’ development. With the mobile app team leaders can easily keep up to date about actual changes. For detailed analytics there is the team leader dashboard.

Team leaders

The mobile app to keep team leaders up to date

For team leader’s there is the team’s section in the mobile app. Here team leaders can easily follow their team’s and employees development. Also creating new teams, inviting new users and managing them can all be done in the mobile app.

Learn more

The dashboard for detailed analytics

The dashboard is the tool for team leaders to get all out of the feedback data.

Follow team’s development

Team leaders can easily follow how their teams are doing. In which teams do people feel happy and productive and in which teams maybe don’t. How does the long term development of these teams look like and what could be the reason for it?

Follow employee’s development

Team leaders can easily follow how their people are doing. Who is doing well and who maybe needs some support? For example: What do Peter’s colleagues think about Peter at the moment? How do they see Peter’s productivity, how his attitude and team working?

Compare team’s development

Comparing the development of many teams is simple. Just select 2-6 teams from the sidebar and start comparing. In which team’s people feel confident with their team leader in which team’s maybe not. How does the long term development of these teams look like. What could team A maybe learn from team B.

Improvement ideas

Team leaders can easily collect their people’s opinions how their teams could be developed. What could we do better in this team? So, team leaders will get a lot of improvement ideas from their people - A great new idea source.

Manage easily

All kind of administration is as simple as possible. Creating teams, creating feedback routines, adding rights, managing team settings… All can be done with just a few clicks.

Viewing rights

For every team can be defined a group of users with viewing rights. These users have then access to follow the development of this team. For example Peter is team leader of the Marketing team and in addition to that he has viewing rights to the website and content creation teams.
